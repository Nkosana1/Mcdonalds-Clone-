The McDonald's Legacy

What started as a single restaurant in San Bernardino, California, has grown into a global phenomenon, serving billions of people worldwide. At McDonald's Times Square, we carry forward the vision of our founders: to provide convenient, affordable, and delicious food to everyone, everywhere.

Our iconic Golden Arches stand as a symbol of comfort, familiarity, and quality in the bustling heart of Manhattan. No matter where you're from, when you walk through our doors, you'll find the same great taste and warm welcome that has made McDonald's a beloved part of communities worldwide.
